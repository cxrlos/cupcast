\begin{abstract}
The forecast: per-match probabilities and expected scorelines for all matches of the
2026 FIFA World Cup, advancement probabilities for all 48 teams, and the podium
distribution from 50{,}000 Monte Carlo tournament simulations under the Dixon--Coles
model \cite{dixoncoles1997} of Paper~I, validated in Paper~II. Includes sensitivity analysis to key-player
availability and a comparison against bookmaker-implied probabilities.
\end{abstract}
