## Context that actually moved a prediction
- **Mexico–Ecuador:** the only real home-plus-altitude edge — Mexico City at 2240 m, played by Mexico, against an unacclimatised Ecuador. This is the host/altitude signal the model was built to capture.
- **USA–Bosnia:** genuine home edge (Santa Clara).
- **South Africa–Canada is NOT a Canada home game** — it's at SoFi Stadium in Los Angeles. Canada is favoured on merit (67%), not on host advantage. Canadian *venues* (Toronto, Vancouver) host other ties (Portugal–Croatia, Switzerland–Algeria), but Canada the team plays in the US.
